Chicken Soup

This recipe is adapted from "Downshiftology," written by Lisa Bryan, and found on https://downshiftology.com/recipes/chicken-soup

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 4 Medium Carrots, Peeled And Sliced
  • 3 Parsnips, Peeled And Sliced
  • 3 Celery Ribs, Sliced
  • ½ Medium Onion, Diced
  • 1 Leek, Halved Lengthwise, Sliced, And Rinsed
  • 4 Garlic Cloves, Minced
  • 1 Teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • ½ Teaspoon Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • 2 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • 2 Sprigs Fresh Thyme
  • 2 Sprigs Fresh Tarragon
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 5 Cups Low-Sodium Chicken Broth
  • ¼ Cup Roughly Chopped Fresh Parsley

=

Directions

  1. Saute the veggies in the oil for 4-5 minutes
  2. Poach the chicken by adding the chicken, broth, and spices for 15 minutes
  3. Break up the chicken with the wooden spoon
  4. Serve and enjoy
